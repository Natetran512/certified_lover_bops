Album Reviewer

So as many of you may know, I'm a pretty big music nerd. Specifically, I listen to a lot of kpop. With the summer comebacks going on, my group of friends decided to rank/score each song of comebacks (e.g. fromis_9, chungha, wjsn, etc). One of my friends ended up finding an article that listed the supposedly "25 best kpop albums of the 2010s." He sent it to me asking for my opinion, to which I responded with I haven't heard it or it shouldn't be there.

With that, we decided to rank those albums ourselves to see if they truly deserved the spot of top album of the decade. It started with just albums from the list, but we began to pick albums that we wanted to personally review as well as take requests from our friends. Through this process I've discovered some songs that I like that I never knew about before. Also I solidified that NCT's sticker is a NOT GOOD out of 10. I'm probably gonna spend a good amount of the summer just reviewing albums with my friend for fun because I enjoy that typa thing.
